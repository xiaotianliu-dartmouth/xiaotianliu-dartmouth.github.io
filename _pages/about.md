Hi, I'm Xiaotian Liu, a third-year Ph.D. candidate at Dartmouth College, advised by [Dr. Yaoqing Yang](https://sites.google.com/view/yaoqingyang). My research interests include scientific machine learning, generative models, reinforcement learning for algorithm discovery, and optimization theory.

Before coming to Dartmouth, I received both my B.S. in Mathematics and Computer Science and my M.S. in Computer Science from Wake Forest University — Go Deacs! — where I was advised by [Dr. Grey Ballard](https://ballard.wfu.edu/).

Outside of research, I enjoy snowboarding and am currently learning how to ski.
